How to Play

Dandelion places flowers on a 5x5 board, trying to fill the entire board with dandelions and seeds.

Wind blows after each placement, spreading seeds from all dandelions in one of 8 directions (N, S, E, W, NE, NW, SE, SW). Each direction can only be used once.

Dandelion wins if the board fills completely. Wind wins if all 7 turns pass without filling the board.
